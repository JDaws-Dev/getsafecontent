## Application Process

### Step 1: Register Online
- URL: https://fpea.com/webforms/2026-exhibitor-registration

### Step 2: Provide Information
- Company name and contact details
- Select zone (1, 2, or 3)
- Number of booths
- Corner upgrades (if desired)
- Booth layout description
- Sign exhibitor agreement

### Step 3: Payment
- Full payment required at registration
- Booth not assigned until payment received
- Early application = better placement

### Step 4: Approval (for new exhibitors)
- FPEA reserves right to reject any company
- May request material samples before approval
- Mail samples to: FPEA, 255 East Drive, Suite E, Melbourne, FL 32904

## Exhibitor Rules Summary

### Allowed
- Distribute catalogs, brochures, samples
- Demo your products
- Collect contact info from attendees
- Play music (with licensing and written authorization)

### Not Allowed
- Subletting booth space
- Displaying products outside your normal business
- Photographing other booths
- Loud speakers without approval
- Distributing food/beverage (unless it's your product)

### Florida Tax Requirement
If selling tangible goods, register with Florida Department of Revenue: 800-352-3671
