### 1. Southeast Homeschool Expo (HIGHEST VALUE)
- **Why:** Perfect target audience - homeschool parents actively choosing tools for kids

- **When:** Saturdays 8am-12pm, May 9 - October 31

- **Why:** 40,000+ attendees, major community event

- **When:** September 19-20, 2026
- **Apply:** January - March 31
