## Top 3 Recommendations

### 1. Southeast Homeschool Expo üèÜ (HIGHEST VALUE)
- **Why:** Perfect target audience - homeschool parents actively choosing tools for kids
- **Cost:** $695 for 10x10 booth (includes 6ft draped table, chair, 8ft back drape, 3ft side drapes)
- **When:** July 24-25, 2026
- **Where:** Cobb Galleria Centre, Atlanta
- **Attendance:** 200+ exhibitors, thousands of homeschool families
- **Optional add-ons:** Sponsorship +$1,000, electricity $95-285
- **Apply:** [homeschoolexpos.com/registration](https://www.homeschoolexpos.com/registration/index.php?route=product/product&product_id=96)
- **Payment:** Check required within 10 days of application

### 2. Suwanee Farmers Market ü•¨ (WEEKLY EXPOSURE)
- **Why:** Low cost, weekly exposure, family-focused crowd
- **Cost:** $250 one-time permit fee per 10x10 space ($500 for double booth)
- **When:** Saturdays 8am-12pm, May 9 - October 31
- **Winter Market:** Two Saturdays/month, 9-11am (November - April)
- **Note:** Application opens January. March meeting mandatory for new vendors
- **Apply:** [Suwanee Farmers Market](https://www.suwanee.com/explore-suwanee/events/farmers-market)
- **Caveat:** Technically for "consumable goods" - may need to pitch as digital wellness

### 3. Suwanee Fest üé™ (HIGH VISIBILITY)
- **Why:** Major annual event, high visibility, booth space provided
- **Cost:** $35 application fee (non-refundable)
- **When:** September 19-20, 2026
- **Hours:** Saturday 9am-7pm, Sunday 12pm-5pm
- **Limit:** Max 3 vendors per category
- **Deadline:** March 31
- **Apply:** [suwaneefest.com/exhibitors](https://suwaneefest.com/exhibitors/)
